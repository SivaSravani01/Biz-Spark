# Biz-Spark
BizSpark is an idea-stage AI platform that helps students generate startup and project ideas based on their skills and interests. It is planned to provide AI suggestions, mentor guidance, project uploads, and certification support for student ideas.
# 🚀 BizSpark

## 💡 AI-Powered Startup Idea Platform for Students

BizSpark is an **idea-stage project** designed to help students generate startup and project ideas based on their skills, interests, and problem areas.

Many students want to participate in hackathons, build projects, or explore entrepreneurship, but they may not know what project to start with. BizSpark aims to provide a simple platform where students can get personalized startup ideas and guidance.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Students often struggle to:

* Choose suitable project ideas
* Find startup ideas based on their skills
* Convert simple ideas into practical solutions
* Get mentor guidance for hackathons and project expos
* Present their projects to potential investors

---

## 💡 Proposed Solution

BizSpark will allow students to enter details such as:

* Technical skills
* Interests
* Preferred domain
* Problem areas
* Team size
* Available tools

Based on this information, the platform can generate startup or project ideas with:

* Problem statement
* Proposed solution
* Target users
* Suggested technologies
* Basic development roadmap

---

## ✨ Planned Features

* 🤖 AI-based startup idea suggestions
* 👩‍💻 Student profile and skill-based recommendations
* 📁 Project idea saving and project uploads
* 🧑‍🏫 Mentor guidance and feedback
* ✅ Mentor certification for promising ideas
* 💼 Investor section to explore certified student projects
* 🏆 Hackathon and project-expo support

---

## 👥 Target Users

* B.Tech and college students
* Beginners in programming and startup development
* Hackathon participants
* Mentors and faculty members
* Early-stage investors

---

## 🛠️ Planned Technologies

* Flutter
* Dart
* Firebase / Supabase

---

## 📌 Project Status

> 🚧 BizSpark is currently in the **idea and planning stage**.

The future goal is to develop a beginner-friendly platform that helps students turn their skills and interests into practical projects, hackathon ideas, and startup opportunities.
